# GTM Positioning One-Pager

Date: March 9, 2026
Audience: Founder, product, marketing, sales
Purpose: Keep positioning, public copy, and sales language aligned.

## Positioning Sentence

Valdrics is a governed spend control platform for cloud, SaaS, and software that helps teams detect waste, assign ownership, apply policy, and run controlled action with reviewable outcomes.

## Category Claim

Use:
- spend governance platform
- governed spend control
- owner-led spend control
- economic control layer for cloud, SaaS, and software

Avoid leading with:
- generic cloud cost management tool
- all-in-one FinOps leader
- proven category leader
- complete incumbent replacement

## Primary ICP

1. Founder-led or lean FinOps teams with growing cloud and software spend.
2. Mid-market engineering + finance teams that need ownership, review, and execution discipline.
3. Cloud-first teams expanding into SaaS and software governance.

## Secondary ICP

1. Procurement-aware buyers who need a clear enterprise path but do not want a product that starts enterprise-only.
2. Security/platform teams that need reviewable change and spend-control workflows.

## Who Not To Target First

1. Fortune-500 replacement deals driven by incumbent RFP templates.
2. Pure unit-cost analytics evaluations.
3. Buyer motions that optimize only for the widest connector catalog.

## Core Problem Statement

Most teams can see spend movement. Fewer can route it to the right owner, apply the right checks, and keep a clean decision trail across cloud and software environments.

## Message Pillars

1. One governed operating layer
   - Cloud, SaaS, and software decisions share one control loop.
2. Owner-routed action
   - Spend signals do not die in dashboards or Slack threads.
3. Safe, reviewable execution
   - Checks, approvals, and exported records stay attached.
4. Finance-grade outcomes
   - Leadership can review signal, decision, and result in one narrative.

## Recommended Homepage Copy

### Headline
Govern cloud, SaaS, and software spend without slowing delivery.

### Subheadline
Valdrics routes spend signals to the right owner, approval path, and measurable outcome across cloud and software environments.

### Alternate FinOps Hero
Run one governed control loop for cloud, SaaS, and software spend.

### Alternate CFO Hero
Protect margin with governed spend decisions before variance compounds.

## Three Supporting Proof Lines

1. One governed operating layer for cloud, SaaS, and software.
2. Signals move with named owners, approvals, and deadlines.
3. Finance, security, and leadership review one clean decision trail.

## Offer Structure

1. Free
   - prove one governed workflow
2. Starter
   - first team, daily review cadence, initial cross-cloud visibility
3. Growth
   - cross-functional rollout, owner routing, Slack + Jira collaboration, SSO
4. Pro
   - finance-grade governance, APIs, auditability, exports
5. Enterprise
   - SCIM, private deployment, procurement/security customization, unlimited scale

## Sales Language Discipline

Say:
- governed execution
- owner-routed control
- reviewable outcomes
- finance-grade operations

Do not say unless backed publicly:
- industry leading
- best-in-class
- proven ROI at scale
- trusted by leading enterprises

## Current Win Conditions

Valdrics wins when the buyer wants:
1. one shared operating loop for engineering, finance, and operations,
2. stronger accountability after detection,
3. controlled action instead of alert-only visibility,
4. a self-serve path before a formal enterprise review.

## Immediate Proof Priorities

1. Design-partner evidence with named use cases.
2. Real screenshots showing signal-to-owner-to-outcome flow.
3. One concise case study with before/after operational change.
4. Clear rollout narrative for the first production workflow.
